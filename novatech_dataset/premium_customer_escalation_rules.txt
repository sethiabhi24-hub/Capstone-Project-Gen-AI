
NovaTech Premium Escalation Rules

1. Enterprise outages must be acknowledged within 15 minutes.
2. Premium customers bypass Tier-1 support queues.
3. Repeated refund disputes require fraud screening.
4. Security incidents involving administrator accounts require immediate SOC escalation.
5. Customers with annual contracts may receive service credits instead of direct reimbursements.
6. Hardware replacement approval requires diagnostic logs and serial verification.
7. API abuse incidents are escalated to infrastructure engineering.
8. Priority customers located in regulated industries require compliance-aware handling.
